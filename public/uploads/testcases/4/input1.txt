# contest in campuscode college module

## Who can create Contest ?
faculty, Head of subject and head of department

contests created by faculty need approval from HOS and HOD to be reflected in student dashboard
contest created by HOS need approval from HOD
contest created by HOD is auto approved

## fields:
title
description
starting date and time
end date and time
last date for registration
duration (calculate using start and end date and time , no input field)
rules and guideline
problems from college problem bank and global problem bank
created by
created on 
approved by
